Drag ● anchors and ● handles to reshape. Moving an anchor carries its handles. Mirror: dragging a handle also moves its sibling across the anchor — Angle keeps the joint smooth, +Length makes it fully symmetric. Turn edit off to preview the blur/threshold. Re-vectorize to bake a new size/kerning.

Blur softens the letters, then the threshold re-sharpens them — overlapping strokes fuse and corners round off, the way ink blooms. Lower the threshold to fatten, raise it to starve. Edge hardness controls how crisp the cut is (low = soft halo). Font must be installed locally.
